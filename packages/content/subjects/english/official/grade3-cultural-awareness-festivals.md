# Cultural Awareness - Festivals

Generate tasks about English-speaking festivals appropriate for {{age}}-year-old students.

## Task Variations

### 1. Festival Matching
Present pictures or symbols of different festivals. Students match the festival name to the correct image.

| Festival | What do you see? |
|----------|------------------|
| Christmas | A. Pumpkin and costumes |
| Halloween | B. Christmas tree and presents |
| Easter | C. Eggs and bunny |

**Instructions**: Match each festival with what you see. Write the letter (A, B, or C).

### 2. True or False about Festivals
Students read simple statements about festival traditions and mark them as true or false.

**Read each sentence. Write T (true) or F (false):**
1. At Halloween, children say "Trick or treat!" ___
2. People eat turkey at Easter. ___
3. Santa Claus brings presents at Christmas. ___
4. Children hunt for eggs at Easter. ___
5. We make snowmen at Halloween. ___

### 3. Fill in the Festival Name
Complete sentences with the correct festival name from a word bank.

**Word Bank:** Christmas • Halloween • Easter • Thanksgiving

1. At ___________, children dress up in costumes.
2. At ___________, we decorate a tree with lights.
3. At ___________, families eat turkey together.
4. At ___________, we paint colorful eggs.

### 4. Festival Traditions - Multiple Choice
Students choose the correct tradition for each festival.

**What do people do? Circle the correct answer:**

1. At Christmas, people...
   - a) hunt for eggs
   - b) give presents
   - c) wear scary costumes

2. At Halloween, children...
   - a) visit Santa Claus
   - b) go trick-or-treating
   - c) paint eggs

3. At Easter, people...
   - a) decorate pumpkins
   - b) make Christmas cookies
   - c) look for Easter eggs

### 5. Compare Festivals Table
Students complete a comparison table showing what happens at different festivals.

| Festival | Colors | Special Food | Activity |
|----------|--------|--------------|----------|
| Christmas | Red and {{blank}} | Cookies and {{blank}} | Open presents |
| Halloween | Orange and {{blank}} | {{blank}} | Trick or treat |
| Easter | {{blank}} colors | Chocolate {{blank}} | Hunt for eggs |

### 6. Festival Timeline
Put festivals in the correct order throughout the year.

**Write the numbers 1-4 to show the order of these festivals during the year:**
___ Easter (spring)
___ Halloween (fall)
___ Christmas (winter)
___ Thanksgiving (fall)

### 7. Festival Vocabulary Matching
Match festival-related words to their meanings or pictures.

**Draw a line to connect the words:**

Left column:
- pumpkin
- present
- bunny
- costume

Right column:
- A gift you give someone
- An orange vegetable
- What you wear at Halloween
- An animal that brings Easter eggs

### 8. Complete the Dialogue
Fill in missing words about festival celebrations.

**Two children are talking. Complete the sentences:**

Anna: "What's your favorite festival?"
Ben: "I like {{blank}}! I love getting presents."
Anna: "My favorite is {{blank}}. I like dressing up in costumes."
Ben: "Do you celebrate {{blank}}?"
Anna: "Yes! We paint eggs and have an egg hunt."

**Word Bank:** Easter • Halloween • Christmas
