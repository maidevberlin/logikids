**Change these sentences:**

2. I don't like rain. → I {{blank}} rain.

**Word Bank:** don't • like • love • don't like

### 7. Multiple Choice Preferences
Answer questions about likes and dislikes.

**Circle the correct answer for YOU:**

1. Do you like dogs?
   - a) Yes, I do.
   - b) No, I don't.

2. Do you like swimming?
   - a) Yes, I do.
   - b) No, I don't.

3. What's your favorite season?
   - a) I like summer.
   - b) I like winter.
   - c) I like spring.
   - d) I like fall.
