# Writing - Guided Production

Create tasks that help students write short texts using templates, sentence starters, and models.

**Task Requirements:**
- Provide clear templates and structures
- Use familiar text types (invitations, cards, profiles, emails, stories)
- Offer word banks and sentence starters
- Keep texts short (4-8 sentences)
- Make writing purposeful and authentic
- Allow for personalization within structure
- Practice correct spelling and punctuation
- Age-appropriate for 9-10 year olds

**Generate varied task types:**

## Variation 1: Birthday Invitation Template
Complete an invitation using prompts and personal information.

**Example:**

**Birthday Invitation**

Dear {{friend's name}},

You're invited to my birthday party!

**When:** {{day}}, {{date}}, at {{time}}
**Where:** {{place/address}}
**What we'll do:** {{activities}}
**Food:** {{food and drinks}}
**Please bring:** {{what to bring}} / Nothing - just yourself!

Please tell me by {{date}} if you can come!

From,
{{your name}}

## Variation 2: Postcard Writing
Write a simple postcard from a real or imaginary trip.

**Example:**

**Front:** [Picture of {{place}}]

**Back:**
Dear {{name}},

Greetings from {{place}}!

I'm having a {{adjective}} time here. The weather is {{weather}}.

Yesterday I {{past activity}}. Today I'm going to {{activity}}.

The food is {{adjective}}. I really like {{specific thing}}.

I miss you! See you soon!

Love,
{{your name}}

**Word bank:** *wonderful, great, amazing, sunny, rainy, visited, saw, went, try, eat*

## Variation 3: "About Me" Profile
Create a personal profile using guided prompts.

**Example:**

**All About Me**

**Name:** {{your name}}
**Age:** {{your age}}
**From:** {{your town/country}}

**My Family:**
I have {{family members}}. ___________________________________

**My Hobbies:**
I like {{hobby 1}}, {{hobby 2}}, and {{hobby 3}}.

**My Favorites:**
- Food: {{favorite food}}
- Color: {{favorite color}}
- Subject: {{favorite subject}}
- Animal: {{favorite animal}}

**About Me:**
I'm {{adjective}}. I can {{ability}}. My dream is to {{dream}}.

## Variation 4: Email to a Pen Pal
Write an email using sentence starters.

**Example:**

**To:** {{pen pal's email}}
**From:** {{your email}}
**Subject:** Hello from {{your country}}!

Hi {{name}}!

My name is _____ and I'm _____ years old.

I live in _____ with _____.

At school, my favorite subject is _____ because _____.

In my free time, I like to _____.

Tell me about you! What do you like doing?

Write back soon!

{{Your name}}

## Variation 5: Thank You Card
Write a thank you card for a gift or help.

**Example:**

Dear {{name}},

Thank you very much for {{gift/help}}.

It's/You're really {{adjective}}.

I {{what you did with it / how it helped}}.

You're very {{adjective}}.

Thank you again!

Love/From,
{{your name}}

**Prompts:**
- the lovely book / the nice present / helping me with...
- beautiful / useful / kind / helpful
- I really enjoyed reading it / I use it every day / It helped me a lot

## Variation 6: Simple Story Completion
Complete a story by filling in the gaps.

**Example:**

**The Lost Dog**

One {{day}}, I was walking in the {{place}}. Suddenly, I saw a {{adjective}} dog. The dog was {{action}}.

The dog looked {{emotion}}. I went to the dog and {{action}}. The dog {{reaction}}.

I looked at the dog's collar. It said "My name is {{name}}. Call {{phone number}}."

I called the number and {{what happened}}. The owner was very {{emotion}}.

In the end, {{ending}}.

**Word bank:** *Saturday, park, small, sitting, lost, happy, petted, wagged its tail, came quickly, happy, everything was fine*

## Variation 7: Weekend Report
Write about your weekend using prompts.

**Example:**

**My Weekend**

Use these sentence starters:

**Saturday:**
- In the morning, I _____
- At lunchtime, I _____
- In the afternoon, I _____

**Sunday:**
- First, I _____
- Then, I _____
- Later, I _____

**My favorite part:**
My favorite part of the weekend was _____ because _____.

**Activities:** *played football, visited my grandparents, watched a film, went shopping, did my homework, met my friends*

## Variation 8: Recipe Writing
Write a simple recipe using a template.

**Example:**

**How to Make {{dish}}**

**You need:**
- _____
- _____
- _____
- _____

**What to do:**

1. First, _____
2. Then, _____
3. Next, _____
4. After that, _____
5. Finally, _____

Enjoy your {{dish}}! It's {{adjective}}!

**Example:** Fruit salad, sandwich, chocolate milk, simple snack

## Variation 9: Pet or Animal Description
Write about a pet or favorite animal using a framework.

**Example:**

**My Pet / My Favorite Animal**

I have a {{animal}} / My favorite animal is the {{animal}}.

{{His/Her/Its}} name is _____. {{He/She/It}} is {{age}} old.

{{He/She/It}} is {{color}} and {{size}}. {{He/She/It}} has {{features}}.

{{He/She/It}} likes to _____. {{He/She/It}} eats _____.

I like {{him/her/it}} because _____.

**Features:** *long tail, soft fur, big eyes, sharp teeth, beautiful feathers*
**Actions:** *play, run, sleep, jump, swim*

## Variation 10: Greeting Card Messages
Write messages for different occasions.

**Example:**

**Birthday Card:**
Happy Birthday, {{name}}!

I hope you have a {{adjective}} day!

Enjoy your {{presents/party/cake}}.

Have fun!

From, {{your name}}

---

**Get Well Card:**
Dear {{name}},

I'm sorry you're {{ill/not feeling well}}.

I hope you feel better soon.

Get well quickly!

From, {{your name}}

---

**Holiday Card:**
Dear {{name}},

Happy {{holiday}}!

I hope you {{wish}}.

See you soon!

From, {{your name}}

**Occasions:** Birthday, Get Well, Christmas, Easter, Good Luck, Congratulations
