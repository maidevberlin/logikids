### 3. Describing Animals
Complete sentences describing animals using adjectives.

**Fill in the blanks with describing words:**

**Word Bank:** big • small • fast • slow • soft • loud

1. The elephant is {{blank}}.
2. The mouse is {{blank}}.
3. The rabbit is {{blank}} and fluffy.
4. The turtle is {{blank}}.
5. The dog can be {{blank}}.
6. The cheetah is very {{blank}}.

### 4. Animal Abilities
Match animals with what they can do.

**Draw lines to match:**

| Animal | What can it do? |
|--------|-----------------|
| bird | A. It can swim. |
| fish | B. It can fly. |
| dog | C. It can jump and hop. |
| rabbit | D. It can run and bark. |

### 5. Animal Body Parts
Label animal body parts.

**Complete the sentences:**

**Word Bank:** tail • ears • legs • wings

1. A dog has four {{blank}}.
2. A bird has two {{blank}}.
3. A rabbit has long {{blank}}.
4. A cat has a {{blank}}.

### 6. Animal Sounds

**What sound does each animal make?**

| Animal | Sound |
|--------|-------|
| dog | A. meow |
| cat | B. woof / bark |
| bird | C. oink |

### 7. My Favorite Animal Description
Complete a paragraph about a favorite animal.

**Write about your favorite animal:**

My favorite animal is a {{blank}}.
It is {{blank}} and {{blank}}.
It can {{blank}}.
I like it because {{blank}}.

**Helpful words:**
- *Colors:* brown, white, black, orange, gray
- *Size:* big, small, tiny
- *Actions:* run, jump, swim, fly, play

### 8. True or False about Animals
Read statements about animals and mark true or false.

**Write T (true) or F (false):**

1. A fish can fly. ___
2. A dog is a pet. ___
3. A cow lives on a farm. ___
4. A bird can swim in the water. ___
5. A cat says "meow." ___
6. An elephant is small. ___
