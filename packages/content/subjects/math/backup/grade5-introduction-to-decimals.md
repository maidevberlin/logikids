### 2. Converting Fractions to Decimals

**Common conversions:**
- Easy: $\frac{1}{2}$, $\frac{1}{4}$, $\frac{3}{4}$, $\frac{1}{5}$, $\frac{2}{5}$

- Hard: 0.625, 0.875, 0.65, 0.68, 0.84

### 4. Comparing Decimals
