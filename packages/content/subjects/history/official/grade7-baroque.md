name: Baroque Art and Architecture
description: Baroque art and architecture as cultural and political expression, buildings as symbols of power from Greek theater to modern government buildings, and the connection between artistic style and political ideology
grade: 7
focus: Architecture and art as political expression across eras
difficulty: medium
learning_objectives:
  - Identify characteristic features of Baroque art and architecture
  - Analyze how buildings express political power and ideology across different eras
  - Compare architectural styles from Greek antiquity through Baroque to modern times
  - Evaluate how rulers used palaces and public buildings to demonstrate authority
  - Explain the connection between Baroque style and absolutist political systems
  - Interpret architectural elements as political symbols and messages
  - Trace the evolution of political architecture from Greek democracy to modern democracy
problem_types:
  - Architectural feature identification
  - Political symbolism analysis
  - Cross-era comparison
  - Building purpose evaluation
  - Stylistic element interpretation
  - Ruler intention assessment
  - Historical context connection
  - Visual source analysis
  - Chronological development
  - Ideology-architecture relationship
difficulty_guidelines:
  easy:
    - Recognize basic Baroque architectural features from images or descriptions
    - Identify which buildings were built in which historical periods
    - Describe the primary function of famous political buildings
  medium:
    - Analyze how specific architectural elements communicate political messages
    - Compare how different political systems expressed power through architecture
    - Explain connections between Baroque style and absolutist ideology
  hard:
    - Evaluate how architectural choices reflected and reinforced political systems
    - Assess continuities and changes in political architecture across multiple eras
    - Synthesize insights about the relationship between form, function, and ideology

  - grade7-absolutism

anti_patterns:
  - Avoid requiring memorization of specific building dimensions or construction dates
