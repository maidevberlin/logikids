---
id: grade3-speaking-presentations
name: Speaking and Presentations
description: Construct logical contributions, use appropriate volume and pace, prepare presentations with notes, and apply standard and academic language
grade: 3
focus: Oral presentation and structured speaking
difficulty: medium
learning_objectives:
  - Construct logical and impactful contributions when sharing experiences or information
  - Use appropriate volume, pace, and intonation during oral presentations
  - Prepare presentations using notes and practice beforehand
  - Incorporate standard German and academic language appropriately
  - Support spoken content with meaningful gestures and body language
  - Organize ideas coherently with clear beginning, middle, and conclusion
  - Adapt speaking style to different audiences and purposes
problem_types:
  - Presentation preparation
  - Volume and pace adjustment
  - Note-taking for speeches
  - Logical argument construction
  - Academic language use
  - Gesture coordination
  - Audience adaptation
  - Structured narration
  - Information delivery
difficulty_guidelines:
  easy:
    - Simple 2-3 minute presentation on familiar personal topics
    - Basic note cards with key words or simple phrases
    - Standard German with minimal academic vocabulary
  medium:
    - 3-5 minute presentation combining personal and factual information
    - Structured notes with main points and supporting details
    - Mix of standard and subject-specific academic language
  hard:
    - 5-7 minute presentation on researched topic with clear structure
    - Detailed notes with transitions, examples, and conclusion
    - Consistent use of academic language and formal presentation conventions
prerequisites:
  - grade2-retelling-stories
  - grade1-speaking-conversation
real_world_context:
  - Presenting a book report to the class using note cards and visual aids
  - Explaining a science experiment or project to classmates step-by-step
  - Sharing information about a hobby or interest during show-and-tell
  - Delivering morning announcements or class news to peers
  - Presenting research findings about an animal or historical figure
anti_patterns:
  - Avoid topics requiring specialized knowledge beyond Grade 3 level
  - Don't expect presentations longer than 7 minutes for 8-9 year olds
  - Avoid formal academic registers appropriate only for older students
  - Don't require memorization - presentations should use notes
  - Avoid scenarios with hostile or challenging audiences
version: 1
version_notes: Initial version based on LehrplanPLUS Bayern Grade 3 speaking and presentation standards
