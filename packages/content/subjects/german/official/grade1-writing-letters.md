---
id: grade1-writing-letters
name: Writing Letters
description: Learn letter formation and handwriting basics including uppercase and lowercase letter recognition, letter sequencing, and distinguishing similar letters
grade: 1
focus: Letter formation and handwriting basics
difficulty: easy
learning_objectives:
  - Recognize and name all uppercase and lowercase letters
  - Match uppercase letters to their lowercase equivalents
  - Identify correct letter formation and sequence
  - Distinguish between similar-looking letters (b/d, p/q, m/w)
  - Recognize proper letter positioning and spacing
problem_types:
  - Uppercase-lowercase matching
  - Letter identification
  - Letter sequence ordering
  - Similar letter comparison
  - Missing letter completion
  - Letter position verification
  - Alphabet sequence tasks
difficulty_guidelines:
  easy:
    - Common letters with distinct shapes (A/a, O/o, M/m)
    - Uppercase letter focus
    - Simple alphabetical sequences (3-5 consecutive letters)
  medium:
    - All standard letters including lowercase
    - Mixed case matching and identification
    - Longer alphabetical sequences (5-7 letters)
  hard:
    - Similar-looking letter pairs (b/d, p/q, m/w, n/u)
    - Mixed case sequences with gaps
    - Letters in non-alphabetical contexts
prerequisites: []
real_world_context:
  - Writing your own name correctly on schoolwork and artwork
  - Labeling drawings and personal belongings
  - Recognizing letters in books, signs, and classroom materials
  - Creating name tags and labels for classroom organization
  - Writing simple messages to family and friends
anti_patterns:
  - Avoid cursive writing or complex handwriting styles
  - Don't include letters from other alphabets or special characters
  - Avoid abstract letter concepts; focus on recognition and matching
  - Don't combine reading comprehension with letter recognition tasks
  - Avoid words in letter recognition tasks; focus on individual letters
version: 1
version_notes: Initial concept for German Grade 1 letter writing foundation
---
