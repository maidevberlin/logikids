**Create a task where {{student_name}} plans, prepares, or practices a presentation.**

Your task should:
- Focus on one of these variations:
  1. **Presentation Planning**: Create a detailed outline/structure for a 5-minute presentation (introduction, main points, conclusion)
  2. **Note Card Creation**: Prepare effective note cards with keywords (not full sentences) to guide free speaking
  3. **Visual Aid Design**: Plan visual supports (poster, slides, photos, objects, diagrams) that enhance understanding
  4. **Introduction Techniques**: Practice strong opening strategies (question, story, interesting fact, problem statement)
  5. **Free Speaking Practice**: Convert full text into keywords and practice speaking freely from notes
  6. **Conclusion Techniques**: Practice effective conclusions (summary, call to action, memorable statement)
  7. **Question Handling**: Anticipate and prepare answers for potential audience questions
  8. **Presentation Evaluation**: Analyze a presentation example and identify strengths and areas for improvement

- Use age-appropriate presentation topics (hobbies, animals, places, historical events, books, sports, technology)
- Show clear presentation structure (Einleitung, Hauptteil, Schluss)
- Provide examples of effective vs. ineffective note cards
- Include tips on body language, eye contact, and speaking clearly
- Use tables to organize presentation outlines or evaluation criteria

**Important**:
- Ensure diverse presentation topics each time (educational, personal interest, informative, persuasive)
- Vary the skill focus (planning, visual aids, speaking techniques, question handling)
- Change the specific topics and contexts
- Provide fresh, engaging presentation scenarios
