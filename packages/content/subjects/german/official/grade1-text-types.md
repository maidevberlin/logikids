---
id: grade1-text-types
name: Text Types & Literary Engagement
description: Understanding different text types (poems, stories, informational texts), analyzing characters and story elements, and presenting texts
grade: 1
focus: Literary genres and text analysis
difficulty: medium
learning_objectives:
  - Distinguish between poems, narratives, and informational texts
  - Describe characters, settings, and actions in stories
  - Sequence story events using visual aids
  - Identify recurring literary elements (rhyme, repetition, rhythm)
  - Make predictions about story developments
  - Connect text information to prior knowledge
  - Present texts through oral reading or dramatization
problem_types:
  - Text type identification
  - Character description
  - Story sequencing
  - Rhyme recognition
  - Prediction making
  - Setting identification
  - Literary element spotting
  - Oral presentation tasks
difficulty_guidelines:
  easy:
    - Single text type with clear visual markers
    - Familiar stories with obvious character traits
    - Simple 3-step story sequences with picture support
  medium:
    - Compare two different text types
    - Describe multiple character traits or story elements
    - 4-5 step story sequences with some text-only information
  hard:
    - Identify genre features across multiple examples
    - Analyze character development or story structure
    - Complex sequencing requiring inference about missing events
real_world_context:
  - Choosing books at the library by recognizing poems, stories, or fact books
  - Retelling favorite stories to family members with correct sequence
  - Identifying rhyming patterns in songs and nursery rhymes
  - Describing characters from bedtime stories (what they look like, how they act)
  - Making predictions while listening to read-aloud stories in class
prerequisites: []
anti_patterns:
  - Avoid texts longer than 100 words for Grade 1 attention span
  - Don't use abstract literary terms beyond rhyme, rhythm, repetition
  - Avoid complex character motivations requiring inference
  - Don't mix multiple text types in one task for easy difficulty
  - Avoid cultural references unfamiliar to 6-7 year olds
version: 1
version_notes: Initial concept for German Grade 1 text types and literary engagement
