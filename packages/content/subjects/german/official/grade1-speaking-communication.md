---
id: grade1-speaking-communication
name: Speaking and Communication
description: Learn to express experiences clearly, follow conversation rules, use polite communication, and respond appropriately in different situations
grade: 1
focus: Oral expression and conversation skills
difficulty: easy
learning_objectives:
  - Express personal experiences and ideas in complete sentences
  - Follow basic conversation rules including turn-taking and active listening
  - Ask clarifying questions when something is not understood
  - Describe objects, events, and situations using appropriate vocabulary
  - Use polite phrases and greetings in different social contexts
  - Respond appropriately to questions and statements from others
problem_types:
  - Conversation rule identification
  - Polite phrase selection
  - Situation-appropriate response
  - Expression matching
  - Sequence description
  - Turn-taking scenarios
  - Greeting selection
difficulty_guidelines:
  easy:
    - Familiar everyday situations (classroom, home, playground)
    - Common greetings and basic polite phrases
    - Simple complete sentences with subject-verb-object structure
  medium:
    - Less familiar situations (visiting doctor, library, store)
    - Varied expressions for the same intention
    - Choosing between formal and informal language
  hard:
    - Complex social situations requiring sensitivity
    - Multiple appropriate response options with subtle differences
    - Conflict resolution and negotiation scenarios
prerequisites:
  - grade1-listening-comprehension
real_world_context:
  - Introducing yourself to new classmates on the first day of school
  - Asking the teacher for help when you don't understand something
  - Sharing your weekend experiences during morning circle time
  - Resolving disagreements with friends on the playground politely
  - Greeting the school principal or cafeteria staff appropriately
anti_patterns:
  - Avoid situations unfamiliar to 6-7 year olds
  - Don't use dialect or regional variations in formal contexts
  - Avoid ambiguous scenarios where multiple responses are equally valid
  - Don't include abstract emotions difficult for young children to express
  - Avoid complex social hierarchies or formal business situations
version: 1
version_notes: Initial concept for German Grade 1 speaking and communication skills
---
