Generate a task where {{childName}} practices recognizing, completing, and enjoying rhymes and simple poems.

**Focus areas to choose from:**
- Identifying rhyming words
- Completing rhyming pairs
- Finding rhyming words in poems
- Creating simple rhymes
- Reciting rhythmic verses

## Task Variations

### Variation 1: Rhyme Recognition
Present {{num1}} word pairs. The student must identify which pairs rhyme and which don't.

Example word pairs:
| Word Pair | Rhyme? |
|-----------|---------|
| Haus - Maus | ✓ |
| Ball - Baum | ✗ |
| Katze - Tatze | ✓ |
| Sonne - Tonne | ✓ |
| Mond - Mund | ✓ |

### Variation 2: Complete the Rhyme
Provide {{num1}} short rhyming couplets with the last word missing. The student must choose or write the correct rhyming word.

Examples:
- *Die Katze trägt eine ___* (Tatze / Mütze)
- *Ich sehe eine kleine Maus, sie wohnt in einem ___* (Haus)
- *Der Ball ist rund, der Hund ist ___* (bunt / gesund)
- *Die Sonne lacht, es ist Tag, nicht ___* (Nacht)

### Variation 3: Rhyme Matching Game
Show {{num1}} words in one column and {{num1}} rhyming words in another column (mixed order). The student must match each word with its rhyming partner.

Column A | Column B
---------|----------
Haus | Nacht
Maus | Tonne
Sonne | Auto
Nacht | Haus

### Variation 4: Find the Rhyming Words
Present a simple poem or verse. The student must identify and underline or circle all the rhyming words.

Example poem:
*Es war einmal ein Mann,*
*der hatte einen Hahn.*
*Der Hahn, der krähte,*
*der Mann, der säte.*

Rhyming words: *Mann - Hahn*, *krähte - säte*

### Variation 5: Odd One Out
Show {{num1}} groups of words where 2-3 words rhyme and one doesn't. The student must identify which word doesn't belong.

Examples:
- *Haus, Maus, Ball, raus* (Ball doesn't rhyme)
- *Katze, Tatze, Matze, Sonne* (Sonne doesn't rhyme)
- *Bär, mehr, sehr, Ball* (Ball doesn't rhyme)

### Variation 6: Create Your Own Rhyme
Provide a target word. The student must think of {{num1}} words that rhyme with it.

Examples:
- Word: *Bär* → Rhymes: *mehr, sehr, Heer*
- Word: *Nacht* → Rhymes: *Macht,acht,acht*
- Word: *Ball* → Rhymes: *all, Stall, Fall*

### Variation 7: Rhyming Poem Completion
Present a simple 4-line poem with {{num1}} missing rhyming words. The student fills in words that make sense and rhyme.

Example:
*Ich bin klein,*
*mein Herz ist ___* (rein / fein)
*Soll niemand drin wohnen*
*als ___ allein.* (Jesus / du)

### Variation 8: Clap and Say Rhythm
Provide a simple rhythmic verse. The student reads it aloud while clapping the rhythm.

Examples:
- *Eins, zwei, drei, vier, fünf, sechs, sieben, eine alte Frau kocht Rüben.*
- *Backe, backe Kuchen, der Bäcker hat gerufen.*
- *Hoppe, hoppe Reiter, wenn er fällt, dann schreit er.*

Mark the stressed syllables with **bold** to guide the rhythm.
