---
id: grade12-facharbeit
name: Research Paper (Facharbeit)
description: Writing extended academic research papers (10-15 pages) with systematic research, academic methodology, and oral defense
grade: 12
focus: Extended academic research and scholarly writing
difficulty: hard
learning_objectives:
  - Develop original research questions within a defined academic scope
  - Conduct systematic research using library databases and academic sources
  - Structure extended papers with clear thesis, methodology, and argument progression
  - Apply scholarly conventions consistently throughout a 10-15 page paper
  - Integrate primary and secondary sources into cohesive academic arguments
  - Defend research findings and methodology in oral presentations
  - Revise and refine extended academic work based on feedback
problem_types:
  - Research question formulation
  - Literature review compilation
  - Thesis development
  - Methodology description
  - Source integration
  - Argument structuring
  - Citation consistency
  - Defense preparation
  - Revision planning
  - Abstract writing
difficulty_guidelines:
  easy:
    - Formulate narrow research questions with guidance
    - Create basic literature reviews with 5-8 sources
    - Structure papers with clear section divisions and linear progression
  medium:
    - Develop complex research questions balancing scope and depth
    - Synthesize 10-15 sources into thematic literature reviews
    - Integrate multiple theoretical perspectives and methodological approaches
  hard:
    - Formulate original research questions contributing new insights
    - Critically evaluate and synthesize 15+ sources across multiple disciplines
    - Navigate methodological complexity and defend analytical choices
real_world_context:
  - Writing a Facharbeit on Kafka's narrative techniques for your German literature course
  - Researching the impact of social media on political discourse for social studies
  - Investigating renewable energy policy in Germany for an environmental science paper
  - Analyzing gender representation in contemporary young adult literature
  - Studying the architectural evolution of Gothic cathedrals for art history
  - Researching the ethics of artificial intelligence in healthcare decision-making
  - Examining the causes and consequences of the 1929 stock market crash
  - Investigating language change in digital communication for a linguistics paper
  - Analyzing Romantic poetry's influence on modern songwriting
  - Studying the psychological effects of social isolation during pandemics
  - Researching sustainable urban planning strategies in European cities
  - Examining the role of propaganda in totalitarian regimes for history class
  - Investigating genetic modification and ethical considerations in agriculture
  - Analyzing the philosophy of existentialism through Sartre and Camus
  - Studying migration patterns and their economic impact on German society
anti_patterns:
  - Don't accept vague research questions without defined scope and parameters
  - Avoid literature reviews that merely list sources without synthesis
  - Don't allow personal opinion to replace evidence-based academic argument
  - Avoid mixing incompatible citation systems throughout the paper
  - Don't skip methodology sections or fail to justify analytical choices
prerequisites:
  - grade11-wissenschaftliches-schreiben
  - grade11-literary-interpretation
  - grade10-material-based-writing
