---
id: grade1-speaking-conversation
name: Speaking & Conversation Skills
description: Narrating experiences, following conversation rules, discussing learning processes, and giving constructive feedback
grade: 1
focus: Oral communication and dialogue
difficulty: easy
learning_objectives:
  - Narrate personal experiences clearly and in logical sequence
  - Follow basic conversation rules including taking turns and listening attentively
  - Participate appropriately in various conversation contexts
  - Articulate observations and thoughts about learning processes
  - Request and provide appreciative, constructive feedback
  - Speak with appropriate pauses and move toward standard German pronunciation
problem_types:
  - Experience narration
  - Turn-taking practice
  - Topic maintenance
  - Feedback formulation
  - Learning reflection
  - Conversation evaluation
  - Opinion sharing
  - Question-response pairs
difficulty_guidelines:
  easy:
    - Single conversational exchange or simple personal experience
    - Common everyday topics familiar to 6-7 year olds
    - Clear, concrete conversation rules (raise hand, wait turn)
  medium:
    - Two to three conversational exchanges with topic consistency
    - Mix of familiar and new conversation contexts
    - Constructive feedback using sentence starters
  hard:
    - Extended dialogue with multiple participants
    - Evaluating whether conversation rules were followed
    - Reflecting on own learning progress and setting goals
real_world_context:
  - Sharing weekend experiences during morning circle time in class
  - Discussing classroom rules and how to participate respectfully
  - Talking about favorite books or stories with classmates
  - Giving compliments and helpful suggestions on peers' work
  - Explaining to parents what was learned at school today
anti_patterns:
  - Avoid adult-level vocabulary or abstract discussion topics
  - Don't require written responses for oral communication tasks
  - Avoid scenarios involving conflict resolution without teacher guidance
  - Don't expect perfect grammar - focus on communication effectiveness
  - Avoid conversations requiring more than 3-4 exchange turns
version: 1
version_notes: Initial version based on LehrplanPLUS Bayern Grade 1 speaking and listening standards
---
