---
id: grade1-reading-short-sentences
name: Reading Short Sentences
description: Reading and understanding simple sentences
grade: 1
focus: Sentence-level reading comprehension
difficulty: medium
learning_objectives:
  - Read simple sentences with comprehension
  - Match sentences to their meaning in pictures or contexts
  - Identify who performs actions in sentences
  - Follow simple written instructions
  - Recognize sentence boundaries using capitals and periods
problem_types:
  - Sentence-picture matching
  - Instruction following
  - Sentence verification
  - Word ordering to form sentences
  - Comprehension questions
difficulty_guidelines:
  easy:
    - Use 3-5 word sentences with simple subject-verb structure
    - Include only common vocabulary from daily life
    - Use clear, unambiguous sentence meanings
  medium:
    - Use 5-7 word sentences with one conjunction (und, oder)
    - Include adjectives to describe nouns
    - Use varied sentence structures
  hard:
    - Use 7-10 word compound sentences with conjunctions
    - Include less common vocabulary
    - Use sentences requiring inference about meaning
prerequisites:
  - grade1-reading-simple-words
real_world_context:
  - Reading simple notes from parents or teachers
  - Understanding written classroom rules and instructions
  - Following recipe instructions with pictures in cookbooks
  - Reading captions and dialogue in picture books
  - Understanding signs and labels in the environment
anti_patterns:
  - Avoid sentences longer than 10 words
  - Don't use abstract or unfamiliar vocabulary for 6-7 year olds
  - Avoid complex grammar like relative clauses or passive voice
  - Don't create sentences with ambiguous pronoun references
  - Avoid scenarios requiring cultural knowledge beyond a child's experience
version: 1
version_notes: Initial version based on KMK Grade 1 standards
---
