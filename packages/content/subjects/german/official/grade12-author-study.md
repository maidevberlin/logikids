name: Author Studies
description: In-depth study of major German authors - biographical context, career development, scholarly positions
grade: 12
focus: Comprehensive author analysis and literary biography
difficulty: hard
learning_objectives:
  - Analyze an author's biographical context and its influence on their work
  - Trace the development of themes and style across an author's career
  - Understand an author's position within literary movements and debates
  - Engage critically with scholarly interpretations of an author's work
  - Develop original scholarly positions on an author's oeuvre
  - Connect an author's work to historical and cultural contexts
  - Evaluate an author's lasting influence and reception history
problem_types:
  - Biographical influence
  - Career development
  - Thematic evolution
  - Stylistic analysis
  - Movement positioning
  - Reception history
  - Scholarly debate
  - Cross-work comparison
  - Historical contextualization
  - Influence tracing
difficulty_guidelines:
  easy:
    - Focus on single major works with clear biographical connections
    - Identify obvious thematic patterns across 2-3 works
    - Recognize basic facts about author's life and historical period
  medium:
    - Analyze relationships between multiple works from different periods
    - Trace subtle developments in style or perspective over time
    - Engage with one or two scholarly interpretations critically
  hard:
    - Synthesize complex biographical, historical, and textual evidence
    - Develop original arguments about author's development or significance
    - Navigate competing scholarly interpretations with nuanced positions
