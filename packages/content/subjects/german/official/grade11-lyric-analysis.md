  - Write extended poetry analyses with thesis-driven arguments

  - Epoch identification
  - Cross-epoch comparison

  - Motif tracing

difficulty_guidelines:
  easy:
    - Clear epoch markers and straightforward themes
    - Standard verse forms with regular meter and rhyme
    - Explicit imagery with identifiable meaning
  medium:
    - Mixed epoch characteristics requiring nuanced analysis
    - Irregular or experimental forms with justified interpretation
    - Multi-layered symbolism requiring contextual knowledge
  hard:
    - Subtle epoch influences or transitional period poetry
    - Complex intertextual references across literary history
    - Dense philosophical or abstract content requiring deep analysis
