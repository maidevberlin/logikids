grade: 10
focus: Historical development of the German language
difficulty: medium
learning_objectives:
  - Trace the development of German from Old High German to Modern High German
  - Understand the significance of Luther's Bible translation for language standardization
  - Recognize historical language features in authentic texts from different periods
  - Understand the social and political factors that influenced standardization processes
  - Connect language history to broader social and cultural developments
  - Analyze how language change reflects and shapes society
  - Compare historical German texts to identify phonological and grammatical changes
problem_types:
  - Period identification
  - Text comparison
  - Feature recognition
  - Standardization analysis
  - Luther's influence
  - Sound shift examples
  - Historical context
  - Language evolution
difficulty_guidelines:
  easy:
    - Identify basic differences between modern and historical German
    - Recognize major language periods when given clear examples
    - Understand Luther's Bible as a key standardization event
  medium:
    - Analyze specific phonological or grammatical changes across periods
    - Explain how social factors influenced language development
    - Compare texts from different periods to identify systematic changes
  hard:
    - Evaluate the relationship between language change and social transformation
    - Analyze competing theories about language standardization processes
    - Synthesize multiple factors that shaped Modern High German
