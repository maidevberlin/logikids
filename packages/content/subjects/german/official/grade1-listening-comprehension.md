---
id: grade1-listening-comprehension
name: Listening Comprehension
description: Understand spoken language by following oral instructions, comprehending short stories, and identifying main ideas and details
grade: 1
focus: Understanding and processing spoken language
difficulty: easy
learning_objectives:
  - Follow multi-step oral instructions accurately
  - Identify main ideas in short spoken texts
  - Answer questions about stories heard
  - Distinguish important information from supporting details
  - Recall specific details from spoken content
problem_types:
  - Instruction sequencing
  - Story comprehension
  - Detail recall
  - Main idea identification
  - Information extraction
  - True-false verification
  - Question answering
difficulty_guidelines:
  easy:
    - 2-step instructions with familiar vocabulary
    - Stories with simple plots and clear sequence
    - Questions about explicit information directly stated
  medium:
    - 3-step instructions with some new vocabulary
    - Stories with minor subplots or character interactions
    - Questions requiring basic inference from context
  hard:
    - 4+ step instructions with complex sequences
    - Stories with abstract themes or less common vocabulary
    - Questions requiring deeper comprehension and connection-making
prerequisites: []
real_world_context:
  - Following classroom directions from the teacher during lessons
  - Listening to bedtime stories and answering questions about characters
  - Understanding safety instructions from adults at home or school
  - Participating in group activities by listening to game rules
  - Following cooking or craft instructions read aloud by parents
anti_patterns:
  - Avoid vocabulary beyond 6-7 year old comprehension
  - Don't use ambiguous instructions with multiple interpretations
  - Avoid stories longer than 5-6 sentences at this level
  - Don't combine multiple unrelated ideas in one instruction
  - Avoid cultural references unfamiliar to young children
version: 1
version_notes: Initial concept for German Grade 1 listening comprehension skills
---
