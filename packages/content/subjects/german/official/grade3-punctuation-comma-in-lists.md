# Punctuation - Comma in Lists

Create a task about using commas in lists for {{age}}-year-old {{gender}} at difficulty level {{difficulty}}.

## Task Variations

Choose ONE of these variations:

1. **Adding Missing Commas**
   - Present 5-7 sentences without commas in lists
   - Students add commas in the correct positions
   - Examples: "Ich mag Hunde Katzen Vögel und Fische.", "Wir spielen laufen springen und tanzen."
   - Include lists with 3-5 items each

2. **Comma or No Comma?**
   - Show sentences and ask whether a comma is needed at marked positions
   - Include both lists (need commas) and non-lists (no commas)
   - Examples: "Ich esse gern Äpfel [?] und Birnen.", "Tim [?] und Tom spielen."
   - Explain the difference

3. **Writing Lists**
   - Provide topics and students write sentences with lists
   - Topics: favorite foods, hobbies, school subjects, animals
   - Sentences must include at least 4 items with correct comma placement
   - Example: "In meiner Schultasche habe ich Bücher, Hefte, Stifte und ein Lineal."

4. **Error Correction**
   - Present sentences with incorrectly placed commas in lists
   - Students identify and correct the errors
   - Include: missing commas, extra commas, commas before "und"
   - Examples: "Ich mag rot blau, und grün." → "Ich mag rot, blau und grün."

5. **"Und" Rule Practice**
   - Focus on the rule: no comma before "und" in simple lists
   - Present lists with and without "und"
   - Students decide where commas go
   - Examples: "Brot, Butter, Käse und Milch" vs. "Brot, Butter, Käse"

6. **Creating Shopping Lists**
   - Students write a shopping list as a sentence
   - Must include items from different categories
   - Example: "Mama kauft Brot, Milch, Äpfel, Tomaten und Schokolade."
   - Include at least 5-6 items

7. **Adjective Lists**
   - Practice commas in lists of describing words
   - Examples: "Der Hund ist groß, braun, freundlich und verspielt."
   - Students complete sentences with adjective lists
   - Include 3-4 adjectives per sentence

8. **Activity Lists**
   - Describe what someone does using lists of verbs
   - Examples: "Am Wochenende will ich lesen, spielen, schwimmen und malen."
   - Students write their own activity plans with commas
   - Include 4-5 activities
