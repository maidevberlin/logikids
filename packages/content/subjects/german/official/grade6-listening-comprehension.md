---
id: grade6-listening-comprehension
name: Listening Comprehension
description: Understanding spoken texts, extracting information, identifying speaker intentions
grade: 6
focus: Oral communication and active listening
difficulty: medium
learning_objectives:
  - Listen attentively to conversation partners to grasp content and intention
  - Extract essential information from age-appropriate spoken texts
  - Understand and summarize main points from audio sources like radio and podcasts
  - Follow complex oral instructions and explanations
  - Distinguish between factual information and opinions in spoken language
  - Recognize speaker's tone, mood, and purpose in various contexts
problem_types:
  - Main idea identification
  - Detail extraction
  - Speaker intention analysis
  - Instruction sequencing
  - Fact vs opinion distinction
  - Spoken content summarization
  - Tone and mood recognition
difficulty_guidelines:
  easy:
    - Short spoken texts (1-2 minutes) with clear structure
    - Explicit information stated directly by speaker
    - Single speaker with neutral tone
  medium:
    - Longer texts (3-5 minutes) requiring inference
    - Multiple speakers with different perspectives
    - Implicit meanings requiring context understanding
  hard:
    - Complex arguments with nuanced positions
    - Evaluating speaker reliability and credibility
    - Recognizing persuasive techniques and rhetorical strategies
prerequisites:
  - grade4-advanced-listening
  - grade3-listening-comprehension
real_world_context:
  - Following classroom discussions and teacher explanations to participate effectively
  - Understanding podcast episodes about topics of interest like science, history, or sports
  - Listening to friends' stories and responding appropriately to their emotions
  - Following spoken instructions for group projects or team activities
  - Evaluating information from audio news reports or educational videos
anti_patterns:
  - Avoid texts with highly specialized vocabulary beyond grade 6 level
  - Don't use audio scenarios unfamiliar to 11-12 year olds
  - Avoid ambiguous questions with multiple valid interpretations
  - Don't require memorization of excessive details from long passages
  - Avoid cultural references specific to regions outside German-speaking areas
version: 1
version_notes: Initial version based on KMK Bildungsstandards 2022 and Bayern LehrplanPLUS
---
