---
id: grade1-reading-simple-words
name: Reading Simple Words
description: Decoding and recognizing simple words through blending sounds, sight word recognition, and word-picture matching
grade: 1
focus: Decoding and word recognition
difficulty: easy
learning_objectives:
  - Blend sounds together to read simple CVC words
  - Recognize and read common sight words automatically
  - Match written words to corresponding pictures
  - Identify syllable structure in written words
  - Self-correct when a decoded word does not make sense
problem_types:
  - CVC word decoding
  - Sight word recognition
  - Word-picture matching
  - Syllable identification
  - Word completion
  - Sound blending
  - Word verification
difficulty_guidelines:
  easy:
    - Use 3-4 letter CVC words with simple consonants and short vowels
    - Include high-frequency sight words (der, die, das, ist, und, ein)
    - Provide clear picture-word associations
  medium:
    - Use 5-6 letter words with common patterns (au, ei, eu, ch, sch)
    - Mix sight words with decodable words
    - Include two-syllable compound words
  hard:
    - Use longer words with multiple syllables (up to 3 syllables)
    - Include less common spelling patterns and letter combinations
    - Require discrimination between similar-looking words
real_world_context:
  - Reading name tags and labels on classroom materials
  - Identifying words on product packaging at the grocery store
  - Reading simple signs and posters in the school environment
  - Recognizing familiar words in picture books and beginning readers
anti_patterns:
  - Avoid words with silent letters or irregular spellings beyond grade level
  - Do not use abstract vocabulary unfamiliar to 6-7 year olds
  - Avoid word pairs that differ by only one letter in easy tasks
  - Do not mix uppercase and lowercase within the same word
  - Avoid compound words longer than 8 letters total
version: 1
version_notes: Initial version - foundational reading concept for Grade 1
---
