learning_objectives:
  - Master systematic prose interpretation at Abitur examination level
  - Analyze narrative technique comprehensively (perspective, time, structure, voice)
  - Connect micro-analysis of language with macro-analysis of themes and meaning
  - Integrate biographical, historical, and literary-historical context appropriately
  - Develop original thesis-driven interpretations supported by close textual reading
  - Apply theoretical concepts to prose analysis (narratology, genre theory)
  - Write complete interpretations under exam time constraints
problem_types:
  - Narrative perspective analysis
  - Temporal structure examination
  - Character constellation mapping
  - Symbolic network interpretation
  - Language style analysis
  - Motif and theme identification
  - Genre convention application
  - Context integration
  - Thesis-driven interpretation
  - Comparative prose analysis
difficulty_guidelines:
  easy:
    - Shorter prose excerpts with clear narrative structure and explicit themes
    - Straightforward narrative perspective with consistent point of view
    - Direct symbolic elements and obvious motifs easily traceable through text
  medium:
    - Novel chapters requiring analysis of both excerpt and broader work context
    - Complex narrative techniques (shifting perspectives, temporal manipulation)
    - Layered symbolism requiring interpretation across multiple textual levels
  hard:
    - Experimental or modernist prose with ambiguous narrative structure
    - Integration of multiple analytical frameworks (form, content, epoch, biography)
    - Examination-level tasks requiring complete interpretation in limited time
