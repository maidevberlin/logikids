---
id: grade1-letter-sounds
name: Letter-Sound Correspondence
description: Learn to match letters with their sounds, recognize initial sounds in words, and distinguish vowels from consonants
grade: 1
focus: Phonics and sound-letter relationships
difficulty: easy
learning_objectives:
  - Identify the sound each letter makes
  - Match letters to their corresponding sounds
  - Recognize initial sounds (Anlaute) in words
  - Distinguish vowels from consonants
  - Identify sounds at different positions in words (beginning, middle, end)
problem_types:
  - Initial sound identification
  - Letter-sound matching
  - Sound position identification
  - Vowel-consonant classification
  - Sound isolation
  - Letter-to-word matching
  - Multiple sound discrimination
difficulty_guidelines:
  easy:
    - Common consonants (m, n, s, l, r) and short vowels (a, e, i, o, u)
    - Focus on initial sounds only
    - Use common German words with clear pronunciation
  medium:
    - All standard letters including less common ones (x, y, c, v)
    - Long and short vowel distinction
    - Sounds at beginning, middle, and end positions
  hard:
    - Letter combinations (ch, sch, st, sp)
    - Umlauts (ä, ö, ü) and their sounds
    - Words with similar-sounding letters (d/t, b/p, g/k)
prerequisites: []
real_world_context:
  - Using an alphabet chart (Anlauttabelle) to find letters for words you want to write
  - Finding letters in your name and friends' names
  - Playing "I spy" games where you listen for beginning sounds
  - Labeling classroom objects by identifying their starting letter sounds
  - Recognizing letters on signs, posters, and packaging in everyday life
anti_patterns:
  - Avoid words unfamiliar to 6-7 year olds
  - Don't mix letter names with letter sounds (use sounds only)
  - Avoid complex consonant clusters at this level
  - Don't use words with silent letters or irregular pronunciations
  - Avoid abstract concepts; use concrete, picturable words
version: 1
version_notes: Initial concept for German Grade 1 phonics foundation
---
