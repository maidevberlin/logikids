  - Master length markers and vowel length indicators in complex multi-syllable words

  - Spell similar-sounding words correctly through grammatical and contextual analysis
  - Spell common foreign words correctly by recognizing origin-specific patterns
  - Use spelling strategies including derivation, word lengthening, and pluralization for verification

  - Consonant doubling decisions

  - Homophone distinction
  - Foreign word spelling
  - Word derivation

  - Error correction
  - Vowel length marking
  - Nominalization identification
difficulty_guidelines:
  easy:
    - Regular consonant doubling patterns with common one-syllable root words
    - Clear das/dass contexts with obvious article or conjunction usage
    - Basic capitalization rules for concrete nouns and sentence beginnings
  medium:
    - Multi-syllable words requiring consonant doubling decisions in derived forms
    - Common homophones requiring grammatical knowledge for correct selection
    - Foreign words from frequent language families with recognizable patterns
  hard:
    - Complex das/dass in relative clauses and conjunctional phrases
    - Edge cases in capitalization including nominalized verbs and adjectives
    - Rare foreign words or words with irregular origin-specific spelling patterns
