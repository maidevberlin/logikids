**Create a task where {{student_name}} learns and practices indirect speech (**indirekte Rede**).**

Your task should:
- Focus on one of these variations:
  1. **Direct to Indirect**: Transform direct speech (quotes) into indirect speech using **dass** clauses
  2. **Konjunktiv I Introduction**: Learn basic forms of **Konjunktiv I** for reported speech (*er sage, sie habe, es sei*)
  3. **Verb Form Changes**: Practice how verb tenses change from direct to indirect speech
  4. **Pronoun Changes**: Adjust pronouns when switching from direct to indirect speech
  5. **Reporting Verbs**: Use appropriate reporting verbs (*sagen, meinen, berichten, erkl√§ren, fragen*)
  6. **When to Use**: Understand contexts requiring indirect speech (news reports, summaries, retelling)
  7. **Without dass**: Practice indirect speech without the conjunction **dass** (requires different word order)
  8. **Mixed Practice**: Convert various types of statements, questions, and commands to indirect speech

- Provide clear examples of both direct and indirect speech
- Use age-appropriate content (everyday conversations, simple statements)
- Show verb form transformations clearly
- Explain the grammatical changes step by step
- Use tables to compare direct and indirect forms

**Important**:
- Ensure diverse speech examples each time (statements, questions, commands)
- Vary the complexity (simple sentences to complex transformations)
- Change the specific content and speakers
- Provide fresh, engaging examples of reported speech
