---
id: grade2-compound-words
name: Compound Words
description: Understanding and forming German compound words by combining two or more words to create new meanings
grade: 2
focus: Word formation and structure
difficulty: easy
learning_objectives:
  - Identify compound words in texts and speech
  - Break compound words into their component parts
  - Form new compound words by combining two base words
  - Understand how meaning changes when words are combined
  - Recognize the main word that determines gender in compounds
problem_types:
  - Splitting compounds
  - Combining words
  - Meaning identification
  - Gender determination
  - Missing component
  - Compound matching
  - Word creation
difficulty_guidelines:
  easy:
    - Use two-part compounds with familiar everyday words (Haus + Tür = Haustür)
    - Include common compounds children encounter daily (Schultasche, Hausaufgabe)
    - Provide compounds where both parts are concrete nouns
  medium:
    - Use three-part compounds with familiar words (Fußballspieler)
    - Include compounds with adjectives or verbs as first component (Schnellzug, Lesebuch)
    - Require understanding of meaning relationships between parts
  hard:
    - Use longer compounds with three or more parts (Geburtstagsgeschenk)
    - Include compounds requiring connecting elements (-s-, -n-, -en-, -e-)
    - Require creating novel but grammatically correct compounds
real_world_context:
  - Reading compound words in storybooks and children's magazines
  - Understanding compound words on signs and labels in everyday life
  - Using compound words when describing objects and activities
  - Recognizing how German creates new words by combining existing ones
  - Learning that the last word in a compound determines its gender
anti_patterns:
  - Avoid compounds with abstract or unfamiliar vocabulary for 7-8 year olds
  - Do not use compounds requiring knowledge of specialized technical terms
  - Avoid compounds with irregular connecting elements beyond basic -s/-n
  - Do not create nonsensical compounds that violate semantic logic
  - Avoid compounds longer than 4 word components total
prerequisites:
  - grade1-reading-simple-words
  - grade1-writing-simple-words
version: 1
version_notes: Initial version - foundational word formation concept for Grade 2
---
