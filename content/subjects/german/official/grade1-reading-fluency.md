---
id: grade1-reading-fluency
name: Reading Fluency & Decoding
description: Developing automatic word recognition through sight words, blending skills, letter combination mastery, and punctuation-guided reading
grade: 1
focus: Reading fluency and decoding
difficulty: easy
learning_objectives:
  - Recognize and read high-frequency sight words automatically
  - Blend individual sounds to decode unfamiliar words
  - Identify and read common letter combinations and digraphs
  - Apply punctuation marks to guide reading pace and expression
  - Read grade-level texts with appropriate speed and accuracy
  - Practice reading strategies with partners or independently
  - Self-monitor reading for accuracy and meaning
problem_types:
  - Sight word recognition
  - Sound blending
  - Letter combination identification
  - Punctuation-guided phrasing
  - Word family matching
  - Reading fluency practice
  - Error detection
  - Reading partner tasks
difficulty_guidelines:
  easy:
    - 5-10 high-frequency sight words from basic vocabulary
    - Simple CVC (consonant-vowel-consonant) words for blending
    - Common two-letter combinations only (e.g., ei, au, eu)
  medium:
    - 10-20 sight words including less frequent terms
    - CCVC or CVCC words requiring multi-step blending
    - Three-letter combinations and digraphs (e.g., sch, ch, ie)
  hard:
    - 20+ sight words in context or mixed with unfamiliar words
    - Complex syllable structures requiring strategic decoding
    - Full sentences with multiple punctuation marks guiding expression
real_world_context:
  - Reading familiar storybooks independently or with family members
  - Recognizing words on street signs, shop names, or product labels
  - Following written instructions for simple games or activities
  - Reading aloud during class reading time or with reading partners
  - Identifying words in favorite songs, poems, or nursery rhymes
anti_patterns:
  - Avoid vocabulary beyond Grade 1 curriculum scope
  - Don't use words with silent letters or irregular spellings at easy level
  - Avoid sentences longer than 8-10 words for fluency practice
  - Don't introduce complex grammar structures requiring advanced comprehension
  - Avoid unfamiliar cultural references or abstract concepts
version: 1
version_notes: Initial version based on LehrplanPLUS Bayern Grade 1 reading standards
