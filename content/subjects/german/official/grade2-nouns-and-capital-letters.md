---
id: grade2-nouns-and-capital-letters
name: Nouns and Capital Letters
description: Recognizing nouns and writing them with capital letters
grade: 2
focus: Noun recognition and German capitalization rules
difficulty: easy
learning_objectives:
  - Identify nouns as naming words for people, animals, and things
  - Recognize that all nouns in German begin with capital letters
  - Distinguish nouns from other word types in simple sentences
  - Apply capitalization rules when writing nouns independently
  - Identify proper nouns (names, cities) and common nouns
problem_types:
  - Noun identification
  - Capitalization correction
  - Noun sorting
  - Capital letter selection
  - Sentence capitalization
  - Noun vs non-noun
  - Proper noun recognition
difficulty_guidelines:
  easy:
    - Use concrete, everyday nouns (Hund, Tisch, Kind)
    - Simple sentences with 3-5 words
    - Clear visual or contextual clues for noun identification
  medium:
    - Mix of concrete and slightly abstract nouns (Freude, Spiel)
    - Sentences with 5-7 words containing multiple nouns
    - Nouns in different sentence positions
  hard:
    - Include proper nouns alongside common nouns
    - Longer sentences (7-10 words) with multiple nouns
    - Less familiar but age-appropriate nouns
real_world_context:
  - Writing stories and labeling pictures in German class requires capitalizing all nouns correctly
  - Reading German books shows that nouns always start with capital letters, unlike in English
  - Learning proper nouns helps children write their own names, friends' names, and places correctly
  - Understanding nouns helps organize vocabulary lists and word sorting activities
anti_patterns:
  - Avoid introducing articles (der/die/das) - focus only on capitalization
  - Don't use plural forms - keep nouns singular for Grade 2
  - Avoid compound nouns (Hausaufgabe) - use simple single-word nouns
  - Don't include abstract nouns beyond simple emotions (Mut, Gl√ºck)
  - Avoid sentences longer than 10 words
prerequisites: []
version: 1
version_notes: Initial version for German Grade 2 curriculum
