# Math Concepts for Grades 1-4 (KMK Standards)

This document identifies 24 coarse-grained mathematical concepts covering the complete German elementary school curriculum (Primarbereich) for grades 1-4, based on the KMK Bildungsstandards and state curricula (Bavaria LehrplanPLUS, NRW).

## Grade 1

### Concept 1: grade1-number-concept-development
- **Title (DE):** Zahlbegriffsentwicklung
- **Title (EN):** Number Concept Development
- **Scope:** Understanding numbers 1-20, counting forwards and backwards, comparing and ordering numbers, recognizing even/odd numbers, place value in tens structure
- **Learning Objectives:**
  - Count objects systematically up to 20
  - Recognize, read, and write numbers 1-20
  - Compare and order numbers using <, >, = symbols
  - Understand place value in the tens structure

### Concept 2: grade1-basic-arithmetic-operations
- **Title (DE):** Grundrechenarten im Zahlraum bis 20
- **Title (EN):** Basic Arithmetic Operations (0-20)
- **Scope:** Addition and subtraction within 20, number bonds to 10, crossing ten, calculation strategies (making 10, near-doubles), word problems, relationship between operations
- **Learning Objectives:**
  - Master addition and subtraction facts to 10
  - Add and subtract within 20 with and without regrouping
  - Apply mental calculation strategies
  - Solve simple word problems involving addition and subtraction

### Concept 3: grade1-geometry-and-spatial-awareness
- **Title (DE):** Geometrie und räumliche Orientierung
- **Title (EN):** Geometry and Spatial Awareness
- **Scope:** Three-dimensional shapes (cube, sphere, cylinder, cone, pyramid), two-dimensional figures (circle, triangle, square, rectangle), spatial relations (above, below, left, right), symmetry exploration
- **Learning Objectives:**
  - Recognize and name common geometric solids and plane figures
  - Describe properties and positions using geometric vocabulary
  - Explore mirror symmetry practically
  - Identify shapes in the environment

### Concept 4: grade1-patterns-and-structures
- **Title (DE):** Muster und Strukturen
- **Title (EN):** Patterns and Structures
- **Scope:** Identifying, continuing, and creating patterns with shapes, colors, and numbers; recognizing repeating and growing patterns
- **Learning Objectives:**
  - Recognize repeating and simple growing patterns
  - Continue given patterns logically
  - Create own patterns with various materials
  - Describe pattern rules verbally

### Concept 5: grade1-introduction-to-measurement
- **Title (DE):** Einführung in das Messen
- **Title (EN):** Introduction to Measurement
- **Scope:** Comparing and measuring lengths (cm, m), telling time to hour and half-hour, understanding money values (coins and bills), time concepts (days of week, daily routine)
- **Learning Objectives:**
  - Compare and measure lengths using standard units
  - Read analog clocks showing hours and half-hours
  - Recognize and compare common coins and bills
  - Sequence daily activities and name days of the week

### Concept 6: grade1-data-and-probability-basics
- **Title (DE):** Daten und Wahrscheinlichkeit - Grundlagen
- **Title (EN):** Data and Probability Basics
- **Scope:** Collecting data from immediate environment, creating tally charts and simple tables, probability language (certain, possible, impossible), simple random experiments
- **Learning Objectives:**
  - Collect and organize data in simple tables
  - Use tally marks and create basic representations
  - Use probability vocabulary in everyday contexts
  - Distinguish between certain and impossible events

## Grade 2

### Concept 7: grade2-numbers-and-operations-to-100
- **Title (DE):** Zahlen und Operationen bis 100
- **Title (EN):** Numbers and Operations to 100
- **Scope:** Place value (ones, tens), comparing and ordering to 100, rounding to tens, addition and subtraction with and without regrouping, mental and semi-written strategies
- **Learning Objectives:**
  - Understand the decimal place value system to 100
  - Add and subtract two-digit numbers using various strategies
  - Round numbers to the nearest ten
  - Check calculations using inverse operations

### Concept 8: grade2-introduction-to-multiplication-and-division
- **Title (DE):** Einführung in Multiplikation und Division
- **Title (EN):** Introduction to Multiplication and Division
- **Scope:** Multiplication as repeated addition and arrays, division as sharing and grouping, times tables for 2, 5, and 10, commutative property, inverse relationship
- **Learning Objectives:**
  - Understand multiplication and division concepts
  - Learn multiplication facts for 2, 5, and 10
  - Recognize commutative property and inverse relationships
  - Solve simple word problems with multiplication and division

### Concept 9: grade2-geometry-and-symmetry
- **Title (DE):** Geometrie und Symmetrie
- **Title (EN):** Geometry and Symmetry
- **Scope:** Detailed exploration of solid properties, recognizing and creating axis-symmetrical figures, mirror lines, symmetry in the environment
- **Learning Objectives:**
  - Describe geometric solid properties using precise vocabulary
  - Identify and create symmetrical figures
  - Find lines of symmetry in figures
  - Create symmetrical patterns and designs

### Concept 10: grade2-measurement-skills
- **Title (DE):** Größen und Messen
- **Title (EN):** Measurement Skills
- **Scope:** Working with meters and centimeters, reading clocks to 5 minutes, calculating time spans, calculating with euros and cents, making change
- **Learning Objectives:**
  - Measure accurately and convert between m and cm
  - Read analog clocks to 5-minute intervals
  - Calculate simple time spans and use calendars
  - Add, subtract, and make change with money

### Concept 11: grade2-patterns-and-data-representation
- **Title (DE):** Muster und Daten darstellen
- **Title (EN):** Patterns and Data Representation
- **Scope:** Arithmetic sequences and pattern rules, creating and interpreting bar charts and pictographs, comparing likelihood of events
- **Learning Objectives:**
  - Recognize and extend arithmetic number patterns
  - Create and interpret bar charts and pictographs
  - Compare probabilities using appropriate language
  - Conduct and record random experiments

## Grade 3

### Concept 12: grade3-numbers-and-operations-to-1000
- **Title (DE):** Zahlen und Operationen bis 1000
- **Title (EN):** Numbers and Operations to 1000
- **Scope:** Place value (hundreds, tens, ones), comparing and ordering to 1000, rounding, mental and semi-written addition and subtraction strategies, estimation
- **Learning Objectives:**
  - Understand place value in the thousands
  - Add and subtract three-digit numbers using various strategies
  - Round to nearest ten and hundred
  - Estimate sums and differences for reasonableness

### Concept 13: grade3-written-addition-and-subtraction
- **Title (DE):** Schriftliche Addition und Subtraktion
- **Title (EN):** Written Addition and Subtraction
- **Scope:** Standard algorithms for addition and subtraction with regrouping, multi-digit calculations, checking results using inverse operations and estimation
- **Learning Objectives:**
  - Apply standard algorithms for addition and subtraction
  - Handle regrouping across multiple places
  - Check results using estimation and inverse operations
  - Solve multi-step word problems

### Concept 14: grade3-multiplication-and-division-mastery
- **Title (DE):** Multiplikation und Division - Vertiefung
- **Title (EN):** Multiplication and Division Mastery
- **Scope:** Mastering all multiplication tables 1-10, division facts, division with remainders, semi-written multiplication, multiplying by multiples of 10, distributive property
- **Learning Objectives:**
  - Recall all multiplication and division facts automatically
  - Divide with and without remainders appropriately
  - Multiply two-digit by one-digit numbers
  - Apply distributive property to simplify calculations

### Concept 15: grade3-arithmetic-laws-and-patterns
- **Title (DE):** Rechengesetze und Zahlenmuster
- **Title (EN):** Arithmetic Laws and Patterns
- **Scope:** Commutative, associative, and distributive properties; arithmetic and geometric sequences; pattern rules and relationships
- **Learning Objectives:**
  - Recognize and apply commutative, associative, and distributive properties
  - Identify pattern rules in sequences
  - Create sequences from given rules
  - Choose efficient calculation strategies using arithmetic laws

### Concept 16: grade3-geometry-and-area
- **Title (DE):** Geometrie und Flächeninhalt
- **Title (EN):** Geometry and Area
- **Scope:** Properties of triangles, quadrilaterals, and circles; drawing with tools; right angles; symmetry (multiple lines, rotational); comparing and measuring area with square centimeters
- **Learning Objectives:**
  - Identify and describe properties of geometric figures
  - Draw figures using ruler and compass
  - Find multiple lines of symmetry
  - Measure area using unit squares (cm²)

### Concept 17: grade3-all-measurement-domains
- **Title (DE):** Alle Größenbereiche
- **Title (EN):** All Measurement Domains
- **Scope:** Lengths (km, m, cm, mm), mass (t, kg, g), volume measures (l, ml), extended time spans (seconds to years), converting units, measuring and calculating
- **Learning Objectives:**
  - Convert fluently between units in all measurement domains
  - Measure accurately with appropriate tools
  - Calculate time spans across hours and days
  - Solve complex problems involving various measurements

### Concept 18: grade3-data-probability-and-combinatorics
- **Title (DE):** Daten, Wahrscheinlichkeit und Kombinatorik
- **Title (EN):** Data, Probability and Combinatorics
- **Scope:** Planning surveys, organizing data in tables and diagrams, fair and unfair games, probability experiments, finding all possibilities systematically, tree diagrams
- **Learning Objectives:**
  - Design surveys and create various diagram types
  - Distinguish fair from unfair probability situations
  - Conduct systematic probability experiments
  - Find all combinations using organized methods

## Grade 4

### Concept 19: grade4-large-numbers
- **Title (DE):** Große Zahlen
- **Title (EN):** Large Numbers
- **Scope:** Numbers to one million, place value understanding, rounding to various place values, comparing and ordering large numbers
- **Learning Objectives:**
  - Read, write, and represent numbers to 1,000,000
  - Understand place value in large numbers
  - Round to various place values appropriately
  - Compare and order large numbers confidently

### Concept 20: grade4-written-multiplication-and-division
- **Title (DE):** Schriftliche Multiplikation und Division
- **Title (EN):** Written Multiplication and Division
- **Scope:** Standard algorithms for multiplication and division, multi-digit calculations, division by one- and two-digit divisors, estimating products and quotients, interpreting remainders
- **Learning Objectives:**
  - Apply standard algorithms for multiplication and division
  - Multiply and divide multi-digit numbers accurately
  - Estimate to check reasonableness of results
  - Interpret remainders appropriately in context

### Concept 21: grade4-algebraic-thinking
- **Title (DE):** Algebraisches Denken
- **Title (EN):** Algebraic Thinking
- **Scope:** Understanding terms and order of operations, solving simple equations, using variables, divisibility rules (2, 3, 5, 10), prime and composite numbers, factors and multiples, functional relationships
- **Learning Objectives:**
  - Evaluate expressions using order of operations
  - Solve simple equations with unknown values
  - Apply divisibility rules and identify prime numbers
  - Recognize and describe functional relationships

### Concept 22: grade4-fractions
- **Title (DE):** Brüche
- **Title (EN):** Fractions
- **Scope:** Understanding fractions as parts of a whole, common fractions (½, ¼, ⅓, ⅕, etc.), comparing and ordering fractions, equivalent fractions, fraction notation
- **Learning Objectives:**
  - Understand and use fraction notation
  - Represent fractions pictorially and symbolically
  - Compare and order simple fractions
  - Find and use equivalent fractions

### Concept 23: grade4-advanced-geometry
- **Title (DE):** Erweiterte Geometrie
- **Title (EN):** Advanced Geometry
- **Scope:** Geometric constructions with ruler, compass, and set square; parallel and perpendicular lines; transformations (translation, reflection, rotation); congruence; solids and nets; area, perimeter, and volume calculations
- **Learning Objectives:**
  - Construct geometric figures using tools accurately
  - Apply geometric transformations to figures
  - Identify and create nets of solids
  - Calculate area, perimeter of rectangles and volume of cuboids

### Concept 24: grade4-data-analysis-and-statistics
- **Title (DE):** Datenanalyse und Statistik
- **Title (EN):** Data Analysis and Statistics
- **Scope:** Analyzing complex diagrams, calculating arithmetic mean, comparing datasets, expressing probability as fractions, comparing theoretical and experimental probability
- **Learning Objectives:**
  - Interpret various complex diagram types
  - Calculate and understand arithmetic mean
  - Compare and analyze datasets
  - Express and compare probabilities mathematically

### Methodology
1. Analyzed KMK federal standards defining competency expectations
2. Examined Bavaria LehrplanPLUS for grade-specific learning objectives
3. Cross-referenced with NRW curriculum for consistency across states
4. Reviewed educational publisher materials for practical topic distribution
5. Identified 60 fine-grained topics from original research
6. Consolidated related topics into 24 coarse-grained concepts for efficient coverage
7. Ensured balanced distribution: Grade 1 (6 concepts), Grade 2 (5 concepts), Grade 3 (7 concepts), Grade 4 (6 concepts)

### Notes
- German elementary mathematics curriculum is organized around five content areas (KMK framework):
  1. Zahlen und Operationen (Numbers and Operations)
  2. Raum und Form (Space and Form)
  3. Muster und Strukturen (Patterns and Structures)
  4. Größen und Messen (Quantities and Measurement)
  5. Daten, Häufigkeit und Wahrscheinlichkeit (Data, Frequency and Probability)

- Number ranges by grade:
  - Grade 1: 0-20
  - Grade 2: 0-100
  - Grade 3: 0-1000
  - Grade 4: 0-1,000,000

- Written algorithms introduction:
  - Grade 3: Addition and subtraction
  - Grade 4: Multiplication and division

- Times tables (Einmaleins):
  - Grade 2: Introduction (2, 5, 10)
  - Grade 3: Complete mastery of 1-10

- All concepts align with KMK Bildungsstandards and are curriculum-compatible across German federal states
